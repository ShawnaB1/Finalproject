The 5 best cycle routes near Amsterdam under 100km

#1 Rondehoep

By far and away the most famous route near Amsterdam! Rondehoep is a short, flat and easy to navigate route through the remarkably quiet rural landscapes just south of Amsterdam. Amsterdam's most famous route takes you along the Amstel river through the charming town of Ouderkerk Aan de Amstel. From Ouderkerk you'll enter a 15km cicular route through farmlands and rural dwellings. A cycling lane exists on the East section of route but stops before Rondehoep West. You'll often encounter vans, trucks and cars on this narrow part of the road so be mindful.

Stop for a coffee at Baker's Out directly after passing under the A10 before heading back to Amsterdam.

Distance:~40km

Terrane Type: flat, paved roads

Top Strava Segments: Ronde Hoep met klok mee (excl Ouderkerk ad Amstel); Ronde Hoep West

#2 Tour de Gooi (partly unpaved)

Heading East out of Amsterdam, this route takes you through the beautiful heather heaths, woodlands and 17th century towns of 'Het Gooi'. First stop is through the village of Muiderberg which has a few coffee shops and restaurants. Next, head on to the beautiful upmarket town of Naarden. Naarden is a fortiffied town notably shapped as a star. You'll then head off-road into the woods and heathlands surrounding Hilsersum.

Past Hilversum you'll find some great pannenkoeken houses in the area of Laag Vuursche so this is a good place to stop.

Distance:~100km

Terrane Type: mostly flat with some climbs, paved and dirt roads

Top Strava Segments: Intra Vreeland; Loenersloot - Abcoude

#3 Waterland Rondje

To the north of Amsterdam is lies the area of Waterland characterized by rolling farm lands, fishing villages and waterways.

The largest town on the route is Monnickendam which is a nice mid-way stop.

Distance:~66km

Terrane Type: flat, paved roads

Top Strava Segments: Einde Durgerdam => Begin Uitdam

#4 Bloemendaal Beach Dunes

You can do this route in either direction but doing it counter-clockwise will allow you to do the most famous 'climb' near Amsterdam...the Kopje van Bloemendaal. It's also a nice route as it avoids Haarlem where there are is a lot of traffic. Once you reach bloemendaal you'll head South West along the dunes.

In summer the beach dunes can be very crowded so the best time for this route is in autumn or winter when the route is relatively empty.

Distance:~95km

Terrane Type: Some climbs and gradients, paved roads and pathways

Top Strava Segments: Het Kopje Van Bloemendaal

#5 Leimuiden Route

Set off in a clockwise direction if you want to hit more Strava segments. This route takes you through Amstelveen before pivoiting right at the Southern tip of on Eastern section of the ringvaart. From here you will ride alone the Westeinderplassen on the the circular canel the 'ringvaart' until you reach Amsterdamse Bos (Amsterdam's forest).

Distance:~61km

Terrane Type: Flat and paved roads and bike paths

Top Strava Segments: Brug Leimuiden Aalsmeerderbrug; Ringvaart Aalsmeerderbrug tot Schiphol (finale)
